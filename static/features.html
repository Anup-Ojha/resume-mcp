Process

From details to PDF in 4 steps

You only do step one. Everything else is automatic.

01

Share your background

Paste your experience in plain text — or upload an existing PDF. Name, roles, companies, skills, education, achievements. Just write naturally; no rigid fields required.

02

Gemini AI structures & writes

Gemini 1.5 Pro parses your input, extracts key data, writes impact-driven bullets, and structures your resume into properly ordered sections following the ATS golden rules.

03

LaTeX template renders

A Jinja2 template injects your structured data into a production-quality LaTeX document. Deterministic layout — no spacing bugs, no runaway lines, no broken tables.

04

PDF delivered instantly

Two-pass pdflatex compilation produces a crisp, properly typeset PDF. Telegram sends it as a file, the web app shows a download link, and MCP returns a URL — all in under 30 seconds.
